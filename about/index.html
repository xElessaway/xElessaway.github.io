About

Ahmed Elessaway documents cyber research in public.

This site is the public desk for writing, dossier building, and OSINT challenge publishing under the identity Ahmed Elessaway (xElessaway).

Approach

The goal is not to make the site feel like a portfolio placeholder. It is meant to function as a working publication where research notes, actor tracking, and challenge design can live beside each other without competing for the same page.

Blog posts capture methods and case notes. Threat intelligence pages act as living dossiers. CTF collections keep the practical side visible and link directly to the supporting challenge material.

Focus Areas

  • Threat actor profiling and ongoing tracking.
  • OSINT workflows, documentation patterns, and investigative tradeoffs.
  • CTF design that preserves learning instead of over-automating the solve path.
